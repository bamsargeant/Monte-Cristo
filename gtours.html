Are Ghosts Real?

Could you spend the night in Australia's most haunted homestead?

Our tour begins with a home-cooked meal followed by a 2 ½ hour tour throughout the main homestead, servants' quarters, stables and ballroom. As you tour this 1880's Victorian homestead by candlelight, you will decide for yourself if the Crawley family still walk the hallways of this magnificent mansion. Hear stories of the past and present while experiencing the energy of the house and it’s spirits. Then, finish the night with delicious dessert, tea and coffee. And finally, it's bedtime for those who dare...
